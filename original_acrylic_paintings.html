Welcome to kuzenny.art

I’m Leonid Kuzenny, — a Ukrainian artist and architect exploring the fusion of minimalism, technology, and coded meaning. This is my direct link to you — no galleries, no intermediaries, no excess. Just authentic work, created and shared from studio to collector.
Home of Meta-Minimalism. — art that decodes the present. Each piece merges structure and silence, algorithm and intuition. UNIX timestamps, QR codes, and binary patterns become part of the visual language — inviting you to read, interpret, and reflect.
Every work is one-of-a-kind. You can explore originals, request a custom piece, or order a hand-painted recreation of previous works. Digital creations are also available as museum-grade prints or high-resolution files for self-printing.
